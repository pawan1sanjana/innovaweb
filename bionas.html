Bio Nas

A Natural Detox Beverage

Made from pineapple peel extract, Bio Nas is rich in antioxidants, eco-innovative, sugar-free, and refreshingly light.

Discover More

Product Overview

Bio Nas is a refreshing natural health drink from pineapple peel extract, enriched with bioactive compounds such as phenolics and flavonoids. With no added sugar, it offers a light, clean taste while promoting wellness and sustainability by valorizing agricultural waste.

Key Benefits

Rich in antioxidants – Contains 14.55 mg GAE phenolics & 5.82 mg QE flavonoids that combat oxidative stress.
Supports wellness – Boosts immunity and may reduce risk of chronic diseases.
No added sugar – Perfect for health-conscious, low-calorie lifestyles.
Eco-innovative – Utilizes pineapple peels, reducing food waste.
Light & refreshing – Hydrating, pleasant for daily use.

Advantages

Affordable functional beverage made from locally available pineapple peels.
Promotes sustainable agriculture & use of fruit by-products.
Naturally free from artificial colors, preservatives & sweeteners.
Appeals to health-conscious and eco-friendly consumers alike.
