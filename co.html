Product Overview

Wel Jelly is an innovative herbal dessert formulated with Welpenala (Cardiospermum halicacabum) extract, enriched with coconut milk and kithul flour. Blended with natural sweeteners and set with gelatin, it combines the healing properties of Ayurveda with the nutritional richness of Sri Lankan ingredients.

Key Benefits

Medicinal Value

Traditionally used to relieve joint pain, inflammation, cough, and asthma. Rich in antioxidants.

Nutrient-rich

Source of healthy fats, lauric acid, and minerals from coconut milk supporting heart health.

Energy Boost

Low glycemic index carbs from Kithul Flour provide steady energy and fiber for digestion.

Balanced Sweetness

Natural sweeteners combined with nutrient-dense flours for a healthier alternative.

Functional Wellness

Medicinal & nutritional benefits delivered in a tasty jelly pudding form.
