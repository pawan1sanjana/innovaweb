What are ProBeads?

ProBeads are tiny, encapsulated spheres packed with billions of live probiotic bacteria. Using extrusion encapsulation, they survive stomach acids and release probiotics in your intestines, supporting gut health, digestion, and immunity.

  • Superior Survival Rate: More live bacteria reach your gut.
  • Targeted Delivery: Probiotics are released exactly where needed.
  • Simple & Effective: Gentle process ensures maximum potency.
