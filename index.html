About INNOVA

INNOVA is the flagship innovation platform by the Agrits Society at OUSL, dedicated to fostering creativity and excellence in food technology and agricultural engineering.

Our Vision

To be the leading hub for food innovation in Sri Lanka, inspiring the next generation of agri-tech leaders through cutting-edge research and sustainable solutions.

Our Approach

We blend academic excellence with hands-on experience, encouraging collaboration, experimentation, and entrepreneurship among our members while addressing real-world challenges.
