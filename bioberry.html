Introduction

Bio Berry uses a probiotic-rich solution with Lactobacillus bacteria as a natural preservative. Fresh strawberries are dipped into the solution, forming a thin protective layer that:

  • Acts as a barrier against harmful microbes
  • Maintains proper respiration of the fruit
  • Preserves natural color, texture, aroma, and taste
